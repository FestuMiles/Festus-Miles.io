Our Facilities

All the apartments are two bedroomed with king-sized beds, and AC in the living room and bedrooms. All the apartments are fully furnished and equiped with DSTV(Access package), Netflix, WIFI, and self-catering facilities. You'll also enjoy the cleaning services that are provided on Mondays, Wednesdays, and Fridays. The apartments are ideal for couples, families, or business travelers who want the best experience. Viewing is available by appointment. Don't miss this opportunity to rent a great apartment at a reasonable price!

About Us

We are situated in Lusaka, 7.8 km from Lusaka Golf Club and 12 km from Lusaka National Museum. The nearest airport is Kenneth Kaunda International Airport, which is about 14 km away. At Chichi Luxurious Apartments we offer accommodation on short and medium term stays to clients in need of affordability, privacy and serenity but with high standard in a beatiful and secure gated community with a flora surrounding, park and walkways.
